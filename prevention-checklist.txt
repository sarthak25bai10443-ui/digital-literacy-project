Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist
1) Always check the sender’s email address before clicking on any link.
2)Do not share OTPs, passwords, or bank details with anyone.
3)Avoid clicking on suspicious or unknown links.
4)Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts.
5)Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.
6)Regularly update your apps and devices for security patches.
7)Verify UPI payment requests before approving them.
8)Never scan unknown QR codes for receiving money (this is a common scam).
9)Use only official apps and websites for banking and payments.
10)Report cyber fraud immediately at cybercrime.gov.in or call 1930.
